Who this home is for

Stability, Structure, and Compassionate Support

Life Changing Living welcomes all age adults seeking stability, structure, and compassionate residential support — including those living on a fixed income, rebuilding after incarceration, navigating mental health recovery, or recovering from health challenges or major life transitions.

Our home is designed for residents who are able to live independently day-to-day, with the comfort of knowing support is just a phone call away.

Finding stability

For people ready for a steady, supportive place to land.

Starting fresh

For someone beginning a new chapter with dignity and care.

Rebuilding day by day

For those working toward a more grounded, independent routine.

Growing in recovery

For residents navigating life transitions and seeking a calm environment.

Good to Know

What we don't provide: ongoing medical or skilled nursing care, or daily supervision for residents who need structured behavioral health support. If you need 24/7 medical supervision or active supervision, we'll help you find the right resources.
